Exquisite Italian Fare Crafted with Passion A Symphony of Flavors: Pizzas Nothing says authentic Italian cuisine like a perfectly crafted pizza. At Bacino Italian Bistro, we take pizza-making seriously. We make each pizza dough from scratch, stretch it by hand until it’s just right, and then bake it in oven. This method gives us a crispy crust that can hold all the delicious toppings without getting soggy.

Variety is Key: Whether you want a classic Margherita or something more adventurous like the Prosciutto e Rucola, our menu has something for everyone. Each pizza has its own unique combination of flavors to satisfy all taste preferences. The secret to our heavenly pizzas lies in our dough-making skills.

We use only the best ingredients, like Caputo flour and fresh yeast, and let the dough ferment slowly for optimal flavor and texture. The result is a light and chewy crust that’s perfect for pizza. “A pizza is only as good as its crust.” – Chef Francesco Sottile Pasta Perfection No Italian meal is complete without pasta.
