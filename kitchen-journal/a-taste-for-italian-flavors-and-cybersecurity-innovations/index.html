A Taste for Italian Flavors and Cybersecurity Innovations

Nestled in the heart of Singapore’s bustling dining scene, Bacino Italian Bistro stands out as a haven for authentic Italian cuisine. It’s here that we caught up with Dr. Magda Lilia Chelly, a name synonymous with cybersecurity excellence and a self-confessed aficionado of Italian gastronomy.

Over a plate of her favorite fromaggio and salumi platter, we delved into her life in Singapore, her groundbreaking work with Responsible Cyber Pte. Ltd., her new third-party risk management solution IMMUNE X-TPRM, and her unwavering passion for both food and cybersecurity.

A Taste of Home at Bacino Italian Bistro

Interviewer: Dr. Chelly, it’s a pleasure to join you in what you’ve described as your favorite Italian bistro in Singapore. What is it about Bacino that keeps bringing you back?

Dr. Chelly: Thank you for joining me! Bacino has a special place in my heart, not just for its exquisite dishes but also for the warmth and authenticity it exudes. It’s like a little slice of Italy right here in Singapore. The food is always fresh, and the ambiance reminds me of the quaint eateries back in Italy. It’s a place where I can unwind and indulge in my love for Italian cuisine.

Building a Career and a Life in Singapore

Interviewer: It’s evident that Singapore has become more than just a place of work for you. How has living here influenced your professional journey and personal growth?

Dr. Chelly: Singapore is a vibrant, multicultural city that’s full of energy and innovation. Living here has been an enriching experience, both personally and professionally. The city’s dynamic tech landscape has provided an ideal backdrop for our work at Responsible Cyber. Singapore’s emphasis on tech innovation and cybersecurity has been incredibly motivating, and it’s been rewarding to contribute to this ecosystem.

The Mission Behind Responsible Cyber

Interviewer: Speaking of Responsible Cyber, your company has made significant strides in the cybersecurity and risk management domains. Could you share more about its inception and mission?

Dr. Chelly: Absolutely. Responsible Cyber was co-founded by myself and Mikko Laaksonen, driven by a shared vision to address the evolving cybersecurity and risk management challenges faced by businesses. Registered in Singapore under the number 201616321C and backed by a robust capital, our mission has been to empower organizations to protect their digital assets effectively. With the support of industry giants like Singtel Innov8 and NUS Enterprise, we’ve been able to push the boundaries of what’s possible.

Innovating Third-Party Risk Management with IMMUNE X-TPRM

Interviewer: Your company’s solutions, IMMUNE X-TPRM and IMMUNE GRC, have been described as revolutionary. Could you tell us more about IMMUNE X-TPRM and its impact on the industry?

Dr. Chelly: Certainly. IMMUNE X-TPRM is our answer to the intricate challenges of third-party risk management in the digital age. The solution leverages AI to provide comprehensive insights and management tools, helping businesses navigate the complexities of third-party interactions securely and efficiently. It’s been gratifying to see how IMMUNE X-TPRM has empowered our clients to strengthen their cybersecurity postures and build more resilient digital ecosystems.

Balancing Cybersecurity Innovation with a Passion for Italian Food

Interviewer: It’s clear that your work is not just a profession but a passion. How do you balance this drive for cybersecurity innovation with your personal interests, like your love for Italian food?

Dr. Chelly: For me, passion is the common ingredient in everything I do, whether it’s advancing cybersecurity or savoring the richness of Italian cuisine. I believe in finding joy and fulfillment in all aspects of life. Embracing my interests outside of work, like frequenting places like Bacino, helps me maintain a balance, stay inspired, and bring creativity into my professional endeavors.

Interviewer: Dr. Chelly, your story is a testament to the power of combining professional excellence with personal passions. Thank you for sharing your insights and experiences with us today.

Dr. Chelly: It’s been my pleasure. Thank you for the delightful conversation and the opportunity to share my journey.
