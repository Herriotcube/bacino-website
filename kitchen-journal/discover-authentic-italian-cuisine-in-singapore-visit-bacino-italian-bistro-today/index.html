The Culinary Entrepreneur Behind Bacino

Francesco Sottile, a dynamic entrepreneur from the picturesque city of Turin, Italy, is the heartbeat of Bacino Italian Bistro.

His culinary entrepreneurship and passion for Italian hospitality have brought an authentic slice of Italy to Singapore’s Pasir Panjang. Francesco’s journey began in Turin, where he was immersed in the rich culinary traditions of his homeland. His love for food and hospitality wasn’t just a career choice; it was a way of life.

This deep-rooted passion led him to explore various facets of Italian cuisine, from traditional family recipes to innovative modern dishes. Moving to Singapore was a bold step, yet one that aligned perfectly with his vision. He saw an opportunity to introduce Singaporeans to the genuine flavors and warmth of Italian dining.

Bacino is not just a restaurant; it’s an extension of Francesco’s dedication to culinary excellence. His cooking style combines the rustic charm of Turin with contemporary twists, ensuring every dish tells a story. From wood-fired pizzas that crackle with authenticity to handmade pastas that melt in your mouth, Francesco’s influence is evident in every bite.
