Where to Satisfy Your Cravings for Authentic Italian Food in Singapore

Are you an expat in Singapore with a hankering for delicious Italian cuisine? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll take you on a culinary journey to discover the best places in the Lion City to satisfy your cravings for authentic Italian food. 1. Little Italy Located in the heart of Singapore’s bustling Clarke Quay, Little Italy is a haven for expats seeking a true taste of Italy.

With its cozy ambiance and friendly staff, this charming restaurant offers a wide range of classic Italian dishes made with the freshest ingredients. Indulge in mouthwatering pasta dishes like carbonara, bolognese, and pesto, or treat yourself to a wood-fired pizza topped with gooey mozzarella and savory toppings. Don’t forget to pair your meal with a glass of fine Italian wine for the ultimate dining experience. 2.

Etna Italian Restaurant If you’re looking for a taste of Sicily in Singapore, Etna Italian Restaurant is the place to be. With its rustic decor and warm atmosphere, this gem in Duxton Hill offers an extensive menu of traditional Italian dishes with a Sicilian twist. Start your culinary journey with some arancini or bruschetta, then dive into their delectable mains like osso buco or seafood linguine.

Vegetarians will also find plenty of options, including the popular eggplant parmigiana. 3. La Nonna For a truly authentic Italian dining experience, head to La Nonna in Holland Village. This cozy trattoria is known for its homestyle cooking and warm hospitality, making you feel like you’ve stepped into a traditional Italian grandmother’s kitchen.

Indulge in their signature dishes such as homemade gnocchi, hearty lasagna, and melt-in-your-mouth tiramisu. With their extensive menu and generous portions, you’ll leave La Nonna with a satisfied belly and a smile on your face. 4. Pasta Brava Situated in the vibrant Arab Street neighborhood, Pasta Brava has been a go-to spot for Italian food lovers since 1993.

This family-run restaurant prides itself on serving authentic Italian cuisine with a focus on homemade pasta. From their silky-smooth tagliatelle to their rich and flavorful risotto, every dish at Pasta Brava is a labor of love. Don’t miss their famous tiramisu for dessert – it’s the perfect sweet ending to a memorable meal. 5.

Cicheti If you’re in the mood for a modern twist on Italian classics, Cicheti in Kampong Glam is the place to be. This trendy Italian eatery offers a menu inspired by the flavors of Venice, combining traditional techniques with innovative ingredients. Try their famous cicheti, small plates of Italian tapas, and indulge in their homemade pasta dishes like squid ink spaghetti or truffle mushroom risotto.

Finish off your meal with a refreshing Aperol spritz or a classic Negroni. 6. Bacino Italian Bistro Amazing experience here…..such a hidden gem with authentic Italian vibes. who needs to go to Italy to taste decadent rich Italian cuisine! The risotto was heavenly and groundbreaking carbonara as well. Come here after filming and was a much needed pick me up after a stressful day.

So there you have it – a guide to the best places to satisfy your cravings for authentic Italian food in Singapore. Whether you’re in the mood for a cozy trattoria or a trendy eatery, these restaurants will transport your taste buds straight to Italy. Buon appetito!
